Our Services

---------------

We provide a wide variety of services in different packages in regards to washing your car, some of these services are :

Hand Washing

Hand washing removes dirt in nooks and crannies that automated washes cannot reach.

Tar Removal

This is a special cleaning product to remove tar and often also glue residue.

Interior Cleanup

Cleaning the interior of a vehicle eliminates harmful bacteria that can accumulate on steering wheels, upholstery and dashboards.

Wax Cotting

Keeping a coat of wax on your car will prevent fading, discoloration, oxidation, and light scratches. A fresh coat of wax will have your car looking brand new.

Tyres Dressing

tire dressing helps prevent cracking, fading, and hardening of your tires. Tire dressing also helps revitalize rubber while leaving a rich shine.

Full Body Hand Polish

Decontaminates the paintwork that a bucket and sponge can't remove.Adds gloss and shine to the paintwork, And more.
